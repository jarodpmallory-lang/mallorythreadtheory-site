Mallory Thread Theory – Privacy Policy

Mallory Thread Theory values your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or make a purchase from our Etsy shop.

To fulfill your order, you must provide us with certain information through Etsy, such as your name, email address, postal address, payment information, and the details of the product you're ordering. You may also choose to provide additional personal information if you contact us directly.

Why We Need Your Information & How We Use It

We rely on a number of legal bases to collect, use, and share your information, including:

  • To fulfill your order, such as processing payments, arranging shipping, and providing customer support
  • To communicate with you, including responding to messages or resolving issues
  • To comply with legal obligations, such as tax or business record-keeping requirements
  • To improve our services, when applicable, based on buyer feedback or inquiries

Information Sharing & Disclosure

Your information is shared only when necessary to provide our services, including:

  • Etsy, as our selling platform
  • Shipping and fulfillment partners, such as print-on-demand providers, for order fulfillment

We do not sell or share your personal information for marketing purposes outside of Etsy.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal and tax obligations. We do not keep your information longer than required.

Your Rights

If you reside in certain territories, including the EU, you have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data.
