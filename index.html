Mallory Thread Theory started as a small creative project between the two of us, built around our shared love for dark humor and apparel that says what most people only think.

We create designs with personality: a little sarcastic, a little bold, and made for people who appreciate humor with an edge. What began as a few ideas turned into something we genuinely enjoy building together — from testing concepts to refining designs that feel funny, wearable, and just the right amount of inappropriate.

We're starting small, learning as we go, and putting care into every piece we create. Our goal is to grow thoughtfully while continuing to make dark humor apparel that feels personal, playful, and easy to connect with.

Thanks for being part of the beginning of our journey.

— Mallory Thread Theory
